OUR SERVICES

Covid Screening Test

CTIS is offering COVID-19 nasal and throat swab test at RM250 for self-paying patients. Appointment must be made at least 24 hours prior to the test date. Result will be made available within 24 - 72 hours. All tests will be conducted in-house at CTIS Test Centre.

Book Appointment

Drive-Thru Screening Services

CITS is providing COVID-19 Drive-Thru Screening Services. All screening services is available by appointments only. Appointment must be made at least 24 hours prior to the test date. Result will be made available within 24 - 72 hours. All tests will be conducted drive-Thru at CTIS Test Centre.
